Home

Tired of spending hours on world-building only to end up with NPCs that aren't incredibly detailed? Want to save some time?

Welcome weary travelers, dungeon masters, and game masters... to the solution!

Not sure where to start? Here's a breakdown:

Builder

A multifaceted NPC (non player character) builder. There are several attributes that can be customized. Any attribute that is not specified or disabled will be randomly generated based on the other attributes.

Tables

A collection of random tables related to character creation.

Prebuilt NPCs

A collection of NPCs that are already made and ready to use.


Prebuilt Monsters

A collection of monsters that are already made and ready to use.

Resources

Websites, books, etc. that can be referenced for character creation.

About Me

A short summary of me, myself, and I.

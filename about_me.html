About Me

Timothy Stockton

I have gone by many aliases over the years. You may know me as TC, Pandemica, or Fenwig. As you can tell, I love gaming. Role playing games, or RPGs are among my favorite type of games. This led to my discovery of the tabletop game Dungeons & Dragons and this website you are currently on. I wanted to create a site where you could choose what you wanted for an NPC and leave the rest to chance.

I also enjoy card games (such as Magic the Gathering), programming (multiple languages), streaming (Twitch mostly), and music production (I use both FL Studio and Ableton to make dubstep and other bass music genres). Hopefully you find this website useful, and I wish you all safe travels in this perilous world of ours...
